# Hi, I'm Goodwin 👋

**Co-Founder & CEO at [Crucibel Technology Limited](https://crucibel.org)**

I build software with a long time horizon in mind - systems designed to endure, not just ship. My work sits at the intersection of healthcare, distributed systems and institutional design. At Crucibel, I'm leading the development of **Nova**, a comprehensive healthcare infrastructure platform built for Kenya's health ecosystem. Nova connects clinics,hospitals, pharmacies, laboratories, diagnostic centres, healthcare professionals and patients through a single interoperable system - addressing fragmentation that has long hampered care delivery across the country.
